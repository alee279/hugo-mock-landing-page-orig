Get Started

Open-source MIT Licensed.

Travel Assistant 🛡️

Organize your travel plans for every trip to create the itinerary best suited for you by keeping track of activities, accomodations, and more.

Shared Itineraries 🚀

Create itineraries collaboratively in groups, where members can contribute to each trip and collectively vote on plans for every event.

Calendar View 📈

View and edit your itineraries in calendar or timeline format for a more organized and visual experience.

Map Integration 🦉

View locations and interact with of all the events on your itineraries on a map, and get the distances and travel times between each event.

Trip Cost Estimation 🧰

Get a financial summary of your trip itinerary including total spent, categories, and up to date currency conversion rates.

Personal Recommendations ✨

See what other users are doing on their trips to the same location based on your interests.
